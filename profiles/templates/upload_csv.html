Upload your saved csv for trends plotting:

  • Press Choose File, the File Upload window will open. Navigate to your locally saved csv file. It should be in the folder that is used as a default download folder by your browser. Usually it is the Downloads or Documents folder on your computer.
  • Wait until "No file chosen" changes to the name of your csv file.
  • Then press Plot Trends button.
  • A form to select desired energy will open in the next view.
